The Simpsons Memory Game


Objective:

The goal of the game is to have fun matching pairs of cards featuring your favorite Simpsons characters!


How to Play:

Each turn, select two cards by clicking on them. If they match, they'll be removed from the board. If not, they'll flip back over, testing your memory skills.


Difficulty Levels:

This game offers two levels of difficulty, Choose your challenge!


  • Easy Mode: You have a generous 60 seconds to uncover all pairs. Take your time and enjoy the game!

  • Hard Mode: For a thrilling challenge, you have only 30 seconds. Race against the timer! If you don't find all pairs in time, the game ends.
