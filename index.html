The Simpsons Memory Game


Objective:

The goal of the game is to find all matching pairs of cards.


How to Play:

Turn consists of choosing two cards. If they match, the cards will be removed from the board. If they don't match, they will be flipped back over.


Difficulty Levels:

This game offers two levels of difficulty.


  • Easy Mode: You have 60 seconds to find all the pairs.
  • Hard Mode: You have 30 seconds to find all the pairs. If they are not found within the selected time, the game will end.
